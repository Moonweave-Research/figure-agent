# Briefing — fig3_resistance_mechanism

> **Genre**: Nature Communications main-text explanatory schematic. The figure is a compact,
> three-column causal narrative: **mechanism → qualitative observable → trap-energy landscape**.
> It is not a replacement for the manuscript's numerical transient-current plots. The B-column
> curve deliberately has no ticks, numerical scale, sample series, or fitted value.

## §1. Topic

Applied voltage drives a sign-agnostic carrier through a disordered sulfur-polymer film. Repeated
capture and release, followed by retention in a representative trap, produces transient-current
decay. The qualitative Curie–von Schweidler relation is shown separately so that the reader can
see the observable between the mechanism and the composition-dependent trap-energy landscape.

## §2. Three-column composition

- **(a) mechanism**: biased sulfur-polymer film, vertical energy orientation, and a temporally
  ordered (not spatial-drift) capture/release/retention sequence.
- **(b) qualitative transient response**: one clean, tick-free `I(t) ∝ t⁻ⁿ` curve. It states the
  causal relation `trapping → I↓` and, under applied V, `I=V/R → R↑`; it does not claim measured
  magnitudes, fitted parameters, a time window, or a composition series.
- **(c) trap-energy landscape**: keep the same vertical energy grammar as (a). S60 is a
  representative discrete state set; S80 is a broad continuous support. These are separate
  comparison units, not a spectrum, bar chart, Fourier transform, or overlaid mixture.

## §3. Physics invariants

1. Measurement physics is transient current under applied V: `I(t) ∝ t⁻ⁿ`, with `n∈(0,2)`;
   `I=V/R`, hence `I↓ ⇒ R↑`.
2. `n` is a fitted CvS exponent. Broader energy support is only a qualitative mechanism cue;
   the schematic must never infer a numeric breadth-to-`n` mapping.
3. Carrier polarity is deliberately unspecified. Left-to-right carrier placement in (a) orders
   states in time and never asserts net drift through the film.
4. At low sulfur, traps are discrete; at S80, they are continuous and broad. Do not express this
   as a fixed peak count, a well-depth strength ranking, or verified trap chemistry.

Physics derives from the sulfur-polymer resistance-method documentation named in the authority
