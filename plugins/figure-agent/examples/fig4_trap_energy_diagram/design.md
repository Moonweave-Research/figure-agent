# Design — fig4_trap_energy_diagram

## Concept
Bimodal trap-state energy landscape: shallow tail traps (near Ec) + deep S–S
radical traps (deep in gap), tied to a bimodal DOS g(Et). Real-space levels ==
spectroscopic peaks. Distinct genre from fig1/2/3 → 3rd diagnostic data point;
specifically stresses lever B (relational/quantitative correctness no detector
can check).

## Layout (178mm)
- a (left, x≈2–9.5): vertical energy axis; vacuum / Ec / Ev levels; shallow band
  (teal) just below Ec; deep red levels mid-gap; trapped electrons; capture↓ +
  release↑(slow) arrows; Eg double-arrow.
- b (right, x≈11.3–16.5): bimodal g(Et) curve on the same energy axis; dashed
  connectors from a's levels to b's peaks.

## Lever-B invariants (declared in spec/§6/§7 — a semantic-assertion check would guard)
shallow above deep · Ec above Ev (Eg≈2eV) · DOS bimodal, peaks aligned to levels ·
shallow blue / deep red · don't conflate theory >1 eV with measured 0.74–0.80 eV.
